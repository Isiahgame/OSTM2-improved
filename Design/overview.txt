Design: OSTM-2

One-sentence:
	"An in-browser incremental game focused on character building and exploration."
	Unpacking:
		- in-browser: Playable in a web page, requiring nothing other than javascript enabled
		- incremental: Steadily growing numbers is good. Progress can be made when player isn't actively engaged
		- character building: RPG systems, customization, growth. Crafting an identity for a character that specializes in X. Inspiration: Path of Exile
		- exploration: Discovery of new mechanics that are unveiled over time, through play, and discovery of locations that unlock mechanics

Major features:
	- Procedurally generated map
	- Class change via prestige
